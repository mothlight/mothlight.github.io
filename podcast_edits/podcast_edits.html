Scripts to cut sections out of mp3 files.

Locate the locations of where to start and stop the edits. I recommend using mplayer from the command line, with a modified mplayer_input.conf to change RIGHT seek +5 (5 second jumps).

Then scripts:
cut out from the beginning of a file to 150 (in seconds) from an mp3
usage: cb.sh filename.mp3 150


cut out from 122 to 150 (in seconds) from an mp3
usage: cm.sh filename.mp3 122 150


cut out 150 (in seconds) to the end of the file from an mp3
usage: cm2.sh filename.mp3 150
